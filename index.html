Success Stories

Local Outdoor Advertising Company Sees Huge Growth

We started working with a local outdoor advertising company during the height of the economic slow down. For over a decade they had been a successful company relying mainly on word of mouth and yellow page advertising. However, when the recession hit so did their clients. It jeopardized the future of the company.

We created a new modern website for them that showed off their products and services. We then began to rank the site for the most important keywords. Through analysis of Google trends we determined what their potential customers were searching looking for on the internet. This information included lead to many actionable insights including rebranding some of their most popular services to attract unreached potential. The site now ranks in the first or second position for all of it's keywords and business has gone from a trickle to a steady profitable stream.

National Manufacturer Sees Increased Demand

We started working with a national manufacturer in the construction industry when their site had been devastated by spam and were desperate to rank. This company had once been dominating the search rankings. They ranked for every keyword combination in their industry. Unfortunately a Google updated crushed their site and they disappeared from the top spot. Furthermore, a non-USA based SEO company they hired sold the credentials on for their site to hackers. Their site was infected with SPAM and their rankings tumbled. After cleaning up the spam they still couldn't get the rankings to return. After trying numerous other agencies they weren't seeing any results.

We migrated their site to a new and secure platform to prevent any further SPAM problems. We performed a deep audit of on-site and off-site issues. During the audit we found that many of the practices that the other companies used were hurting their site. Using our SEO strategies the site is now on page one for all of its keywords and has seen an increase in demand for it's products.
